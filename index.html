ROACH MOTEL

Definition:
The Roach Motel dark pattern makes it easy to get into a service, but hard to get out.

How users are trapped:

  • Easy and quick sign-up process
  • Free trial attracts users
  • Cancel option is hidden or less visible
  • Multiple steps required to cancel

👉 Users are encouraged to click Start Free Trial

Why users click the trial

Example:
A subscription service that’s easy to sign up for but requires multiple steps or is hidden when attempting to cancel.

Psychological Tactic:
The user feels trapped in the service.

Impact on Users:

  • Users forget to cancel
  • Unable to cancel in time
  • Unwanted automatic renewals

Solution:
Simplify cancellation to match the ease of signing up.
