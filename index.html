How they trap users

A subscription service that is easy to sign up for but requires multiple steps or hides the cancellation option when users try to leave.

Psychological Tactic: Users feel trapped in the service and may delay or avoid cancelling.

Impact on Users:

  • Users forget to cancel
  • Unable to cancel in time
  • Leads to unwanted renewals and charges

Solution: Simplify the cancellation process so it matches the ease of signing up.
