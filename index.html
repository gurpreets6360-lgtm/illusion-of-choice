ROACH MOTEL

Definition: The Roach Motel dark pattern makes it easy to get into a service, but hard to get out.

How users are trapped:

  • Easy sign-up process
  • Free trial attracts users
  • Cancel option hidden
  • Multiple steps to cancel

👉 Users are encouraged to click Start Free Trial

Why users click the trial

📌 Example: Easy to sign up but hard to cancel.

🧠 Psychological Tactic: Users feel trapped.

⚠️ Impact:

  • Forget to cancel
  • Miss deadline
  • Unwanted charges

💡 Solution: Make cancellation easy.
